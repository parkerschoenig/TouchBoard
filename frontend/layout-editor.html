How to use
  • Click ＋ next to a category to create a widget
  • Place a widget from the sidebar into the layout editor
  • Drag a second widget onto a placed card to stack them (multi-page widgets like NetBox, TrueNAS, Proxmox & Weather can't be stacked)
  • Click ✎ on a card to reorder or remove widgets
  • Drag a placed card back here to remove it
  • Scroll over a card to flip between widgets
  • Changes save automatically
